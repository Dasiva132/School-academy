FAQs

Here are some common questions parents, students, and prospective families might have about our school.

Admissions & Enrollment

How do I apply to School Academy? + -

To apply, please visit our online application portal. Required documents typically include academic transcripts, birth certificate, and immunization records. The application deadline for the upcoming academic year is March 15th. We accept students from age 3 for our pre-kindergarten program up to Grade 12.

What are the admission requirements? + -

Admission requirements vary by grade level but generally include a review of previous academic records, a standardized entrance exam (for grades 5 and above), and an interview with the admissions committee. Recommendations from previous teachers may also be requested.

What is the tuition fee and what does it cover? + -

Our tuition fees vary by grade level. The fees cover academic instruction, textbooks, most school supplies, and participation in standard extracurricular activities. Detailed fee schedules and payment options are available on our Tuition & Fees page.

What is the student-teacher ratio? + -

Our average student-teacher ratio is 12:1, ensuring personalized attention and a supportive learning environment for all students.

Student Life & Well-being

What are the school hours? + -

School hours for all grades are from 8:30 AM to 3:30 PM, Monday through Friday. We have early dismissal at 1:00 PM on the last Friday of each month for faculty professional development.

Is transportation provided? + -

We offer a limited number of bus routes for student transportation. Details on routes, availability, and fees can be found on our Transportation page. Many families also organize carpools, and we have designated drop-off and pick-up zones.

What is your school's policy on bullying? + -

School Academy has a strict zero-tolerance policy on bullying. We promote a safe and respectful environment for all students. Our anti-bullying policy outlines clear reporting mechanisms, disciplinary actions, and support services for victims. Students are encouraged to report any incidents to a trusted adult.

How does the school handle student health and emergencies? + - We have a full-time school nurse on campus to attend to student health needs and administer first aid. In case of a medical emergency, parents/guardians will be contacted immediately. We require up-to-date emergency contact information and medical history for all students.

What is the school's dress code/uniform policy? + - School Academy requires students to wear a school uniform. The uniform policy promotes a sense of community and minimizes distractions. Detailed guidelines on uniforms, including approved vendors, can be found in the student handbook or on our Uniform Policy page.

What are the options for lunch? + - Yes, our cafeteria provides healthy and nutritious lunch options daily. Students can choose from a rotating menu that includes hot meals, salad bar, and vegetarian options. Packed lunches from home are also permitted. Information on meal plans and menus can be found on the Dining Services page.

General Information

How can parents get involved in the school community? + -

We highly value parent involvement! Parents can join our Parent-Teacher Association (PTA) or volunteer for various school events, classroom activities, or committees. Information on how to get involved is regularly shared through our newsletters and parent portal.

How does the school communicate with parents? + -

We maintain open communication with parents through various channels, including our secure online parent portal, weekly newsletters, email announcements, and scheduled parent-teacher conferences. Teachers are also available for individual meetings by appointment.

What security measures are in place at the school? + -

The safety and security of our students are paramount. We have controlled access points, security cameras, and trained security personnel on campus during school hours. All visitors are required to sign in and wear a visitor's badge. We also conduct regular emergency drills.

Where can I find the school calendar? + - The official school calendar, including holidays, events, and important dates, is available on our website's Calendar page. You can also subscribe to our digital calendar for automatic updates.
